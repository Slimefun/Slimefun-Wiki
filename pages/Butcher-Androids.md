### Butcher Android

A specialized android is created to kill a hostile or/and animal mobs.

There are three tiers on butcher android, each with different modifiers listed below.

| Tier | Fuel Efficiency | Damage |
| ---- | --------------- | ------ |
| Basic | 1.0x | 4 |
| Advanced | 1.5x | 8 |
| Empowered | 8.0x | 20 |
